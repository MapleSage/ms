Our Story

Our story started in 2007, when bunch of friends back in NYC decided to explore full potential of managed e-commerce services in India. A service that will enable SME’s to handle every aspect of online business; web-design and execution, inventory management, marketing, order processing and fulfillment and even customer service.

A complete end to end solution that enables them to have an infrastructure that is innovate, inspires constant imitation, and will be quiet frankly, incomparable, and thus iRetail Solutions was born, an end to end e-commerce company that enables SME’s especially fashion retailers and manufacturers, to have an online flagship store in matter of days with minimal upfront cost and no additional infrastructure. iRetail will build it, manage it, run it and even make it profitable on revenue sharing model.

Our offering was unique however little ahead of time for Indian market. In 2007 e-Commerce in India was still its nascent stages and was not prepared for business model like ours. After a struggle of few years and few projects we decided to put iRetail on hold till market is ready.

Recent demonetization presented a new opportunity and made us rethink, SME’s in India are struggling to adapt to rapidly changing business requirement and transition towards digital economy. They are looking for business partner that will help them in this transition at every stage and enable them to navigate through the complexities of their digital journey.

For a company to succeed today, they need to make their online presence felt. In the past, events, conferences, magazines, and advertising could get companies in front of prospective buyers. While these tactics are still important, it is vital that they are supported by a comprehensive digital marketing plan.

And we decided to launch iRetail 2.0 as “MapleSage” an inbound digital marketing agency in partnership with HubSpot, a global leader in inbound marketing.

MapleSage helps SME’s in their move towards digital economy and turn their digital vision into reality. A new kind of agency – one that combines enterprise-level technology implementation, and value-added marketing and analytics services.

MapleSage helps you turn your digital vision into reality, generate leads, close deals, grow revenue and maximize ROI. We deliver a superior customer experience by applying proven Inbound methodology at every stage of customer journey, from discovery to point of sale & after.

We will do it by executing our considerable influence at every step of the online business. Influence that making global reach a realistic and immediate possibility.
And we will do this while defending your business. Your business is sacred. It is everything. At ever consumer touch point, your business needs will lead the charge, maintaining integrity, extend the experience, and forever champion the lifestyle you have created.
We know that Digital Marketing is a new space for many companies. At MapleSage, we love to partner with companies that are ready to dive in and reap the rewards. We’ve put together a team of experts in design, technology, and marketing, so you can focus on the things that uniquely differentiate your business and leave the rest to us.

Some call it growing. Others call it winning. We call it Digital Marketing Evolved.

Let MapleSage do the heavy lifting while you stay focused on your core business – From presence to profit.
